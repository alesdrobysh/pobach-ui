## Design Context

### Users
Native Belarusian speakers and language enthusiasts playing a daily semantic word game. They use it as a grounding, meditative moment, likely during transitions in their day.

### Brand Personality
Intellectual, calm, organic. The interface should feel thoughtful, steady, and unhurried.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Home/Organic**: Inspired by physical materials like paper and print. Warm, grounded, and "homely."
- **Transparency**: Influenced by the precision and clarity of Linear, but adapted to feel more tactile and human.
- **Theme**: Light/Dark modes should remain paper-like/earthy (`#F7F3ED` light, `#1A1410` dark). Avoid harsh, sterile digital aesthetics.
- **Reference**: Linear (for structural clarity), physical printed materials (for aesthetic warmth).

### Design Principles
1. **Tactile Clarity**: Interface elements should feel like they exist in physical space—subtle depth, deliberate alignment, and respectful use of negative space.
2. **Intentionality**: Every element must earn its place. Avoid "noisy" UI patterns.
3. **Calm Hierarchy**: Use typography and spacing to guide the user naturally, rather than demanding their attention through bold color or excessive contrast.
4. **Consistency in Warmth**: Maintain the earthy palette consistently across all states, keeping the experience grounded.
